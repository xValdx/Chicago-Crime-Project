    Usages




    Given the reality of Chicago's crime rate, it is not surprising that some individuals may feel anxious or unsafe about visiting certain destinations in Chicago, especially if they are unfamiliar with the city. A crime radar that displays the number of violent crimes that occurred in the area of interest in the previous year could be a useful tool for these individuals to assess the safety of the area before they go.

    By using a crime radar, individuals can gain a better understanding of the crime situation in the area they intend to visit and make informed decisions about their safety. They can also use the information provided by the radar to take necessary precautions, such as avoiding certain streets or areas or traveling with a group of people.

    This is also a particularly useful tool for current residents. As a resident it is still of paramount importance to be able to make informed decisions about how to travel to destinations and where the safest destinations are.



    Top and Bottom ACT Averaging Schools

    A crime radar that displays the number of violent crimes that occurred in or around a high school in the previous year could be a useful tool for parents, teachers, and students to assess the safety of the school and its surrounding areas. This information can help them make informed decisions about attending or working at the school, and can also help them take necessary precautions to stay safe.

    Furthermore, having a crime radar specifically for the top and bottom performing high schools can be relevant as these schools may face unique challenges. For example, high-performing schools may have more resources to address safety concerns, but may also have a higher likelihood of being targeted by criminals due to their reputation or perceived wealth.

    On the other hand, lower-performing schools may have fewer resources to address safety concerns and may be located in areas with higher crime rates. Having a crime radar can help identify potential safety concerns and allow for proactive measures to be taken to mitigate them.
